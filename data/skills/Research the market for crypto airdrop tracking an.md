## 2. Airdrop Economy Size

- **Total Value**: \$20B+ cumulative; **2024: \$19B**, **2025: \$4.5B** (Berachain \$1.17B, Story \$1.4B).[^2][^1]
- **Avg per User**: \$500-2k for active DeFi (10+ protocols); top farmers \$10k+ (e.g., Jupiter retro \$10k).[^6]
- **Participants**: 1-2M farmers globally; 200k+ BSC via Telegram (gas/time investment \$100-1k/yr).[^6]


## 3. Pain Points

**Manual Work**:

- Scan 20+ sites/wallets for eligibility (DeFiLlama, Twitter, Discord).
- Verify points (LayerZero, zkSync dashboards), bridge/test txs.
- Track 50+ snapshots/deadlines via spreadsheets.
**Time**: **10-15 hrs/week** early farmers; 3-5 hrs optimized.[^7]
**Missed Opportunities**: 70% drops unclaimed (forgot deadlines); avg \$1k/user/year lost (e.g., \$112k Monad gas fail).[^8]

## 5. Improvements \& Complaints

**Current Issues**:

- **Fragmented**: No unified BSC checker (miss 50% chain-specific).
- **Manual Verification**: Paste wallet 10x/day; no auto-qualify sims.
- **Scams**: 30% fake drops; delayed alerts miss snapshots.
- **Gas/Time Waste**: Farm 10 drops → 80% zero; Reddit: "Hours for \$50 worthless tokens".[^6]

**Dramatic Better**:

- **Wallet-Auto-Scan**: Real-time eligibility + claim links (Earni.fi but BSC-optimized).
- **AI Prediction**: "80% claim chance" via TVL/activity.
- **Telegram One-Click**: Alerts + bridge/claim bots.
- **ROI Filter**: Only high-value (\$500+) drops.
