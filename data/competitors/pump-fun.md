## Fee Structure

- Token creation: **miễn phí**
- Bonding curve trading: **1%** (buy + sell)
- Graduation fee: 0.015 SOL one-time
- Post-graduation (PumpSwap): 0.3%
- Liquidity tại graduation: ~$12,000 USD locked
- Creator nhận: **0%** — toàn bộ về platform

## Bonding Curve Mechanics

- Fixed supply: 1 tỷ tokens
- 800M tokens on curve
- Graduation threshold: ~$69,000 market cap
- Post-graduation: move to PumpSwap (own DEX, launched 2025 thay Raydium)

## Timeline

- Launch: 2024
