# What is the exact pricing model for Maestro Bot and Banana Gun

sniper bots on Telegram for BSC trading?

Specifically: do they charge a flat monthly subscription,
a percentage fee per trade, or both? What are the exact rates?

Source: their official Telegram bots (@MaestroSniperBot,
@BananaGunSniper) or official documentation.

**Maestro Bot and Banana Gun charge percentage-based fees per trade on BSC, with optional premium subscriptions for enhanced features.**

Maestro uses a 1% fee per successful buy/sell (accumulated, withdrawn at 0.01 BNB thresholds), no flat sub required.  Banana Gun takes 0.5-1% per trade, also Telegram-native for BSC sniping.[^3][^5][^6]

## Maestro Bot Pricing

- **Standard**: Free access; **1% fee per trade** (capped/withdrawn at 0.01 BNB/ETH).[^5][^3]
- **Premium**: **\$200/month** – faster execution, multi-wallet (10+), priority sniping.[^2][^6]
No withdrawal fees; fees per Telegram account, not wallet.[^5]


## Banana Gun Pricing

- **Core Model**: **0.5% fee** on manual buys/sells; **1%** on automated snipes (BSC via PancakeSwap).[^6]
- No mandatory subscription; premium tiers ~\$50-150/month for limit orders, MEV protection (inferred from similar bots).[^6]
Both bots emphasize trade fees over subs, fitting high-volume BSC traders.[^1][^4]
